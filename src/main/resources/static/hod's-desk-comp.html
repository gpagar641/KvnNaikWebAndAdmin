My Dear Students,
Computer engineering is a discipline that integrates several fields of electrical engineering, E & TC engineering & computer science required to develop computer hardware and software and being applied in every field of engineering & technology. This branch involves many hardwares and softwares aspects of computing, from the design of individual microcontroller, microprocessor, personal computer, and supercomputer, to circuit design. This field of engineering not only focuses on how computer systems themselves work, but also how they integrate into the larger picture. The special areas in computer engineering include coding, cryptography, and information protection, communications & wireless networks, compilers & operating systems, computational science & engineering, computer networks, mobile computing, & distributed systems. Computer systems architecture, parallel processing, robotics, embedded systems, integrated circuits, VLSI design, signal, image and speech processing. Vast opportunities of growth have been thrown open for the computer software and hardware industry, with the globalization of Indian Economy. Software industry is one of the largest growing sector of Indian economy. hey focus on the design of microprocessors, personal computer and supercomputer.
