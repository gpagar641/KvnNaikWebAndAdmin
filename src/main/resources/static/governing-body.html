Governing Body

The functioning of the administration of Loknete Gopinathji Munde Institute of Engineering Education & Research (LoGMIEER) is observed by the Governing Body (GB). The chairman of GB is appointed by the Secretary through a resolution by the Board of Directors, Trustees and Office Bearers of KVN Naik Shikshan Prasark Sanstha. The eminent personality from the field of education, industry and representatives from the Directorate of Technical Education, Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune and Western Regional Officer, Mumbai - All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi are the members, whereas the Principal is ex offico Secretary of LMC.
